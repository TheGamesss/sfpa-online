Super Fancy Pants Adventure is a wildly smooth, hand-drawn 2D platformer where you sprint, wall-jump, grind rails, and slash through imaginative worlds with incredible momentum and fluid animation that still feels unique today. Originally evolving from one of the internet’s most beloved Flash game series, it combines fast parkour-style movement, expressive stick-figure art, and creative level design into a game that feels like skateboarding, platforming, and cartoon chaos all at once.
In case you aren't familiar with the controls, here's the default keyboard controls:
  • Move: Left and Right Arrow Keys
  • Jump: S
  • Duck: Down Arrow
  • Hold down while running to slide, while moving backwards to roll, or while landing to slide boost.
  • Stomp or slide into a baddie to stun them, run into a stunned baddie to kick.
